# dk_kds_README.txt

The files in this section result from the conversion of datasets originating
from [the Danish Agency for Climate Data](https://kds.dk) (previously known as SDFI).

## Included grids

### DVR90 (ensemble) -> EVRF2019

*Source*: [Agency for Climate Data](https://www.klimadatastyrelsen.dk/om-klimadatastyrelsen/vilkaar-og-priser)  
*Format*: GTG  
*License*: [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

Transform DVR90 coordinates (EPSG:5799) to EVRF2019 (EPSG:9389).

* dk_kds_dvr90_evrf2019.tif


### DVR90 (ensemble) -> EVRF2019 (mean tide)

*Source*: [Agency for Climate Data](https://www.klimadatastyrelsen.dk/om-klimadatastyrelsen/vilkaar-og-priser)  
*Format*: GTG  
*License*: [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

Transform DVR90 coordinates (EPSG:5799) to EVRF2019 (EPSG:9390).

* dk_kds_dvr90_evrf2019_mean_tide.tif
